# Daily Organizer

New in version 0.0.5 - May 7, 2023:
- Support for meeting templates!

You can now create custom templates when creating new meeting notes. These templates are stored by default in `~/DailyOrganizer/MeetingTemplates` and are copied into newly-created meeting files.

For more details please consult README.md at https://github.com/mremallin/DailyOrganizer.
